Wisdom from the Bhagavad Gita

श्लोक 2.47:
कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन।
मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥

Meaning: You have a right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions...

Characters from the Bhagavad Gita

1. Lord Krishna

The Supreme Being who imparts wisdom to Arjuna during the Kurukshetra war.

2. Arjuna

A skilled warrior and the prince of the Pandavas, facing a moral dilemma in battle.

3. Bhishma

The granduncle of the Pandavas and Kauravas, renowned for his vow of celibacy.

4. Drona

The royal teacher of the Kauravas and Pandavas, expert in military arts.

5. Sanjaya

The charioteer of Dhritarashtra, narrating the events of the battle to him.
